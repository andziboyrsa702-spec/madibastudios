The Studio

Founded at UCT — Madiba Studios is more than just a barbershop - it is a student-built brand redefining grooming culture on campus. Founded at the University of Cape Town by Motheo Mbulawa, Madiba Studios began as a response to real-life responsibility and has grown into a trusted grooming service for students across UCT, CPUT, and UWC. What started as a mobile operation has evolved into a brand known for consistency, professionalism, and community.


Convenience & Quality — We understand the student lifestyle - tight schedules, limited time, and the need to always look presentable. That is why we bring grooming directly to you. Through our mobile services and growing on-campus presence, we eliminate long queues, reduce travel time, and deliver high-quality cuts at affordable prices.


Hygiene & Professionalism — At Madiba Studios, hygiene and professionalism are non-negotiable. From sanitized tools and clean equipment to branded uniforms and reliable service, every detail is designed to give you confidence in your experience.


Our Vision — Our vision is clear: To become South Africa's leading student grooming brand, expanding across campuses and setting the standard for quality, convenience, and culture. This is not just grooming. This is legacy.

Legacy & Vision

The Beginning — Madiba Studios was founded with purpose, resilience, and vision. What began as a student-led initiative at the University of Cape Town has grown into one of the most recognized student-focused grooming brands in the Southern Suburbs of Cape Town. Built from the ground up, Madiba Studios was created to address a simple but powerful need - accessible, high-quality grooming tailored to student life.


Family & Responsibility — At its core, the business was inspired by responsibility and family. The foundation of Madiba Studios is deeply rooted in supporting my mother and building something meaningful that could create stability, opportunity, and long-term impact. That sense of purpose shaped the discipline, work ethic, and consistency that define the brand today.


Growth & Recognition — Starting as a mobile service, we brought convenience directly to students, eliminating long queues and travel while maintaining professional standards. Through consistency, discipline, and attention to detail, the brand quickly earned trust across campuses, expanding its reach to a growing and loyal client base. Over the past three years, we have maintained an exceptionally high standard of service, with over 90% of customer experiences being positive. Our journey has not gone unnoticed. As a finalist in The Pitch UCT 2025, Madiba Studios has been recognised for its innovation, impact, and potential as a scalable business.


Today — Today, Madiba Studios stands as a structured, evolving brand - combining quality service, strong branding, and a clear growth strategy. We have moved beyond just being a service provider; we are building a system that supports student entrepreneurs, creates opportunities, and sets a new standard for grooming culture. From humble beginnings to a recognized and award-winning presence, our mission remains the same: to deliver premium grooming experiences while building a legacy that extends beyond the chair.

— Motheo Mbulawa, Founder & CEO
