The Studio

Founded at UCT — Madiba Studios is a student-built brand redefining grooming culture on campus. Founded by Motheo Mbulawa, it has grown into a trusted service for students across UCT, CPUT, and UWC.


Convenience & Quality — We understand the student lifestyle: tight schedules, limited time. That is why we bring grooming directly to you. Mobile services, on-campus presence, no long queues.


Hygiene & Professionalism — Sanitized tools, clean equipment, branded uniforms. Every detail designed to give you confidence.


Our Vision — To become South Africa's leading student grooming brand, expanding across campuses and setting the standard for quality, convenience, and culture. This is not just grooming. This is legacy.

Legacy & Vision

The Beginning — Madiba Studios was founded with purpose, resilience, and vision. What began as a student-led initiative at the University of Cape Town has grown into one of the most recognized student-focused grooming brands in the Southern Suburbs of Cape Town.


Family & Responsibility — The business was inspired by supporting family and building something meaningful that could create stability, opportunity, and long-term impact.


Growth & Recognition — Starting as a mobile service, we brought convenience directly to students. Over the past three years, we have maintained an exceptionally high standard of service, with over 90% of customer experiences being positive. As a finalist in The Pitch UCT 2025, Madiba Studios has been recognised for its innovation, impact, and scalability.


Today — Madiba Studios stands as a structured, evolving brand — combining quality service, strong branding, and a clear growth strategy.

— Motheo Mbulawa, Founder & CEO
